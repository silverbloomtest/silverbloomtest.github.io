Features and services

Create a "wow" moment with our solutions and gifts. We know how to make a lasting impression with more than just flowers.

  • Floristics

    Silver Bloom boasts an extensive selection of dried and preserved florals just waiting to be used for your special day. Bouquets, boutonnieres, and centerpieces will be created with individual approach. Choosing dried and preserved will get rid of any watering or wilting worry and leave you with an everlasting piece from your most special day.

  • Gift Decoration

    When it comes to great gifts, we're always thinking of ideas that are outside the box.Share the love and give someone you care about something special with our help. Our selection of gift decoration ideas includes many options for special occasions, holidays, anniversaries, weddings.

  • Design solutions

    The interior of every building and room can tell a lot about their owners and residents, about their taste and views. Our designers will help you personalize your space. We can start from scratch with everything new or incorporate treasured items you already own. Every decorating service is customized to your needs.
